Welcome to Parko, a free Parking-Service by Group 40

Parko is a new and creative service that selects the perfect parking lot FOR YOU. Just sign up, deposit your preferred vehicle and you are immediately able to pre-order for your selected period.

Reservations

Our simple Reservation-Service makes it possible for you to select a period of time in which you want to park your car. Just enter your license plate and Parko will reserve an optimal parking lot for you.
